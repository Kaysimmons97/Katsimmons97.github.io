Welcome to our website!

KAC Pharmacy was designed by Katherine Simmons, Clarence Cloud, and Alexander Stott. As students of the 14th cohort of the Cleveland Codes program, the KAC Pharmacy web application represents the culmination of our coursework. Our web application is a CRM based on the concept of a pharmaceutical company similar to the Amazon subsidiary Pill-Pack. We achieved our CRM model using Python and JavaScript/JQuery to combine our front-end HTML/CSS and back-end SQlite/CSV code. The KAC Pharmacy team made full use of GitHub and Visual Studio Code to manage the creation and modification of files. Having organized all of our files in the shared repository, we were then able to transfer the code into the web2py framework. Our databases were drawn from random data created via the Mockaroo app and read via SQlite into the web2py database framework.
